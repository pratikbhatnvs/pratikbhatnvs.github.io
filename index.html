About Dr. Pratik Bhat

Dr. Pratik Bhat is an MD Pathologist and published novelist based in Goa, India. While professionally trained in hematology, cytopathology, histopathology and laboratory medicine, he is equally known for his emotionally intense and psychologically layered storytelling.

His literary works explore love, betrayal, darkness, vulnerability, and the fragile complexity of human relationships — themes that have earned him a growing readership.
