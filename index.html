About Dr. Pratik Bhat

Dr. Pratik Bhat, MD, is a pathologist, novelist, and poet whose dark and evocative works explore the complexities of love, loss, betrayal, and the deeper layers of human emotion. His writing is known for its psychological intensity, emotional honesty, and unflinching portrayal of flawed relationships and inner turmoil. Among his published works are The Deteriorated, You Cheat On Me, Baby?, Do I Get to Make You Mine?, You Shattered My Soul!, Darker Than Black (series), The Dark Is When I Love, Lies, The Case of a Fragile Mind, and Dr. Bhat’s Book on Immunohistochemistry for Everyday Pathologists.

Beyond his literary and medical pursuits, Dr. Bhat is an avid walker and a devoted coffee enthusiast, often found reading, reflecting, or working on his next book.

While professionally trained in hematology, cytopathology, histopathology and laboratory medicine, he is equally known for his emotionally intense and psychologically layered storytelling.
